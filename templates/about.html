Welcome to Try & Buy

TRY & BUY is your ultimate destination for exploring fashion with convenience. Similar to an in-store changing room, our platform allows buyers to virtually try clothes for the perfect fit. Whether you're looking for T-shirts, Jeans, Sunglasses, or Caps, we've got you covered with the best quality and prices.

Established in 2019, TRY & BUY aims to make every special occasion even more memorable. With a dedicated design team, we bring you the latest fashion trends for every season. Join us as we turn our slogan into action: "TRY & BUY, Walk in for the Fashion, Stay in for the Style."

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize online shopping by providing a virtual trial room that offers users a superior design, quality, and a preview of how the product looks on them. We are committed to offering exceptional services and real value to our customers.
